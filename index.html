Paul Jiang

CS Undergraduate @ Purdue University

I am a senior at Purdue University (John Martinson Honors College) pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics.

My research interests lie in High Performance Computing (HPC), Formal Methods, and Systems. I am particularly interested in characterizing numerical deviations in AI hardware and verifying the correctness of emerging arithmetic realizations.

Currently, I am working with Prof. Ganesh Gopalakrishnan at the University of Utah on formally characterizing Tensor Core arithmetic, and with Prof. Jingbo Wang at Purdue on differential Zonotope-based verification of Vision Transformers.
